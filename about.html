About Regalia Foods

A pantry brand built on FUTA soil.

We started Regalia Foods to solve a simple campus problem: getting honest, good-quality foodstuff without a trip into town or a bargaining fight at the market.

Our story

From market runs to a full pantry brand

Regalia Foods began as a small stall serving students and staff around FUTA Akure who needed beans, garri, flours and fresh produce without the stress of a full market trip. Word spread quickly, and today we run a full catalogue of everyday staples — grains and flours, fresh vegetables, oils, spices and drinks — sold directly on and around campus.

The name "Regalia" is deliberate: we treat every kitchen we supply, whether it's a hostel room or a family home, like it deserves the good stuff. Well-sorted beans, clean rice, fresh-cut ugu — no shortcuts.
